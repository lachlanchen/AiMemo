AI Secretary DNA

Built on the proof-of-concept email triage from AISecretary; now refocused on rich memory ingestion.

Trigger

Inbound email, voice note, or quick chat (WeChat, Teams, or Messenger) is mirrored into AiMemo through IdeasGlass.

Interpret

LLMs (current work inside ../IdeasGlass/backend/memo) normalize the story into intent, tags, and data points.

Store

The assistant writes concise rows into the memo table, keeps running totals, and keeps metadata for experiments.

Respond

AiMemo clarifies next steps, drafts replies, and surfaces signals for your travel, gym, or workday flow.

Design logic

This repo is the launchpad for AiMemo; the IdeasGlass memo project houses the LLM trigger and table export work. AiMemo blends email leadership from AISecretary, itinerary-focused sections, and the memory-first philosophy from IdeasGlass so that you can stay ahead of every plan.

The goal is a single assistant that routes natural language to precise tables, keeps conversations legible, and augments an inbox with actionable insight. memo.lazying.art is the home of that vision.
